# AL Brooks

Author of the three-volume *Reading Price Charts Bar by Bar* series on price action trading:

1. **Trading Price Action Trends** - Trend trading concepts
2. **Trading Price Action Reversals** - Reversal patterns (this source)
3. **Trading Price Action Ranges** - Trading range concepts

## Background

- Written from thousands of hours of trading experience
- Focuses on Emini, futures, and stock trading
- Uses 5-minute candle charts for illustration but discusses daily and weekly charts as well
- Also trades stocks, forex, Treasury note futures, and options

## Trading Philosophy

Believes the market is controlled by institutions running buy and sell programs. Individual traders should think of institutions as any entity that trades enough volume to be a significant contributor to price action.

Core thesis: "The market is always trying to break out, and then the market tries to make every breakout fail."

## Key Contributions

- **Bar counting system**: High 1/2/3/4, Low 1/2/3/4 terminology
- **Always-In concept**: Framework for continuously assessing market direction
- **Sign of strength analysis**: Criteria for identifying high-probability reversal setups
- **First hour trading**: Special focus on the opening range and early session dynamics

## Relationship to Indicators

Spent over 10,000 hours writing and testing indicators and systems before concluding that the best success comes from finding an approach compatible with one's personality. Dismisses the idea that there is a 50% chance of being right - emphasizes that understanding price action provides an edge.
